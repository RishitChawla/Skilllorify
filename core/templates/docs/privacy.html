This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) sets forth the privacy practices of Skilllorify Eduventure Pvt. Ltd. 1. Applicability

This Policy applies to all users of our Services, including learners, trainees, trainers, business partners, government bodies, and visitors accessing or using our website, mobile applications, and other digital platforms.

By accessing our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Personal Information

Full name, contact number, email address, postal address, date of birth, gender, ID proof, photograph, biometric data (if applicable for certification or government program enrolment).

2.2 Academic and Employment Data:

Educational background, employment status, work experience, resume, skill assessment results, certification details.

2.3 Financial Data:

Billing and payment information. We use third-party payment processors, and we do not store your card or banking information directly.

2.4 Technical and Usage Data:

IP address, device identifiers, browser information, location data, pages visited, time spent, cookies, and other similar tracking technologies.

3. Legal Basis for Data Collection

We collect and process personal information only where:

  • It is necessary to perform our contractual obligations with you;
  • You have provided explicit consent (where required);
  • It is required for legal compliance, regulatory reporting, and government scheme participation;
  • It is necessary to pursue our legitimate interests (e.g., improving services, business analytics), provided such interests do not override your rights.

3.2 Improving Our Services

  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Developing new features and services
  • Troubleshooting technical issues
  • Conducting research and analysis

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To register, authenticate, and manage your account;
  • To deliver courses, training, certificates, and learning assessments;
  • To communicate regarding services, fees, offers, and service updates;
  • To verify identity for government or partner programs;
  • For audits, reporting, dispute resolution, and legal compliance;
  • For marketing and promotional communications (with opt-out option);
  • To detect fraud, abuse, and misuse of our systems and services.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data under strict confidentiality with:

  • Government bodies and accreditation authorities
  • Vetted third-party service providers (hosting, customer support, analytics, etc.);
  • Business partners, franchisees, and instructors for operational and support purposes;
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities, if legally required or in good faith belief that disclosure is necessary.

We enter into legally binding agreements with all third-party processors to ensure compliance with privacy norms.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy or as required by applicable laws. When we no longer require the data, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Subject to applicable laws, you may:

  • Request deletion or anonymization of your data;
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential processing;
  • Lodge complaints with a regulatory authority;

8. Data Security

We implement strict technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including firewalls, encryption, access control, periodic audits, and staff training. However, no method of data transmission is 100% secure, and we disclaim liability for data breach beyond our reasonable control.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such websites and encourage you to read their privacy policies before submitting any information.

10. International Transfers

If we process data from users outside India, we ensure compliance with cross-border data transfer regulations and apply adequate protection mechanisms where applicable.

11. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If we discover that we have collected data from a child without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it immediately.

We reserve the right to modify this Policy at any time. Changes will become effective upon being posted on our website. You are advised to review this page periodically for updates.
